why we are the best

We have 30 years experiens cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to quality to deliver innovative solutions for the rubber manufacturing industry

Sai Rubber, with over two decades of expertise, stands as a leader in innovative rubber manufacturing. Guided by the visionary leadership of Mr. Anil Gupta and the dynamic approach of Mr. Sahil Gupta, we specialize in high-quality rubber profiles, molded products, and metal bearings for industries like construction, transportation, and infrastructure.

With a state-of-the-art 150,000 sq. ft. facility in Faridabad and advanced technologies like microwave curing, we ensure reliable, durable solutions tailored to client needs. Even during challenges like the COVID-19 pandemic, Sai Rubber has demonstrated resilience, adaptability, and a commitment to excellence, making us a trusted partner in the industry.
